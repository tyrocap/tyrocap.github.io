'Zero to One' by Peter Thiel

  • Madness is rare in individuals but in groups it is a law. Be contrarian (not for its own sake) and think for yourself.
  • Each successful company is a monology that solved one unique problem. Every other company is one that failed to escape competition.
  • Regarding competition, either don't throw any punches or strike hard and end it quickly. There is no middle ground.
  • Characteristics of monopoly: propr.tech, network effects, economies of scale and branding.
  • You are not a lottery ticket. Be Definite Optimist. (..-> Plan -> Attempt -> Improve ->..)
  • Always remind yourself that Pareto distribution is everywhere (20% will yield 80% outcome)
  • A great company is a conspiracy to change the world. Look for secrets.
  • Beginnings are special. Foundations must be set right.
