'The Bitcoin Standard' by Saifedean Ammous

In the early 1970s, an ounce of gold was ~$30. Today, the figure is in the $1280s. What this means is that in the past 50 years, the value of gold is increased ~40X. Sure, no big deal, right?. After all, everything one can think of has had the similar story in its price journey over the past five decades. Except, if we change our perspective a bit and think about any good in terms of its value instead of its price, things start to get really interesting. So coming back to the gold and its price, what if we look at the problem from the dollar's perspective - that is, instead of the value of an ounce of gold increasing ~40X, what if we say that the value of dollar decreased ~1/40X. This realization might seem an obvious observation for some, but for me, it is one of those 'aha' moments that the first few pages of the selected reading materials provide. And the book is one of those.
